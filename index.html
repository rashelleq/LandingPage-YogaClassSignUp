Classes Offered

Vinyasa yoga

Vinyasa yoga is the synchronizing of physical movement with your breath in hatha yoga is called vinyasa. This technique allows you to create a deeper absorption of awareness through a rhythmic moving meditation while practicing yoga.

Yin yoga

Yin Yoga is slow-paced style of yoga as exercise, incorporating principles of traditional Chinese medicine, with asanas that are held for longer periods of time than in other styles.

Power yoga

Power yoga is a hybrid form of yoga that focuses on building strength and endurance. It was developed out of Ashtanga yoga — another form of “yoga as exercise” popularized in the 80s.
